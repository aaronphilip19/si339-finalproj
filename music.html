My 2023 Top 5 Artists

Drake

    2023 Spotify Rating: #1

    Favourite 2023 Song: Members Only

Drake has been a mainstay in my playlist over the years and has held number 1 ever since I've had Spotify. His classics and a new album every year keep me hooked

Travis Scott

    2023 Spotify Rating: #2

    Favourite 2023 Song: Telekinesis

Travis Scott was bound to be in my top 5 this year with his much-awaited album Utopia. Telekinesis is one of my favourite songs of his all time and I loved his new project.

Metro Boomin

    2023 Spotify Rating: #3

    Favourite 2023 Song: Around Me

This arguably was the year of Metro Boomin. He dominated my playlist at the beginning of the year with Heroes & Villians. His Spiderman Soundtrack playlist had some hits for me, with "Calling" standing out

The Weeknd

    2023 Spotify Rating: #4

    Favourite 2023 Song: Creeping

The Weeknd has been another mainstay of my playlist for years. I often listen to his old track more than his newer ones, specifically from "Beauty Behind the Madness". My unpopular opinion is I don't mind bumping "KPOP" with Travis and Bad Bunny

Future

    2023 Spotify Rating: #5

    Favourite 2023 Song: Telekinesis

I don't remember ever exlusively listening to any new Future songs but I always tend to listen to his old tunes whenever I'm going to class or at the gym. "U are my High" and "Purple Reign" have been go-to songs for me recently.

My top 5 songs of 2023

Telekinesis

    2023 Spotify Ranking: #1

    Artist: Travis Scott

It was no surprise to me this song came out 1st on my Wrapped. I was hooked the moment I listened to this song in July and it's been on repeat ever since. SZA and Travis' hook make the song for me. Shoutout to Future.

Fein

    2023 Spotify Ranking: #2

    Artist: Travis Scott

Fein is the party anthem of 2023 for me. It's definitely a song I put to hype myself up. It's one of my favourites of the album and is a throwback to "Astroworld" Travis.

Meltdown

    2023 Spotify Ranking: #3

    Artist: Travis Scott

Meltdown is another Drake and Travis collab that doesn't fail to disappoint. Drake's rapping on this track is some of his best of late.

Trance

    2023 Spotify Ranking: #4

    Artist: Metro Boomin

As you can see, Travis is really dominating 2023 for me and Trance is one of the go to's from Heroes and Villains for me. I definitely see this song sticking in my rotation for a while.

Fein

    2023 Spotify Ranking: #5

    Artist: Metro Boomin

This song has been in my top 5 for most of the years I've viewed my Wrapped. This is probably my most favourite Drake song of all time. Can play this in any context and vibe to it.
